My Notes ✍️

Helpful tips and tricks for Entry-Label Designers

‍

In some of the startups, I had to play the role of one woman army because of some constraints and this gave me an immense opportunity to touch most of the design areas including trying my hands on in giving life to my designs.

Being from a non design ordinary school, I often struggled with basic things like color selection, nailing that perfect composition using good usage of whitespace, coming up with decent illustration work in my early days for not being an artist, and some more other problems. It was a difficult yet enjoyable journey without having a mentor.

Here I have attempted to gather all the insights from my struggle days that could be helpful for entry label designers, who are basically from non-design schools.
